# Plumbob — Challenge Catalogue

A seed list of challenge concepts organised by category, ready to be written up in the standardised format later. A mix of **established community classics** (e.g. Legacy, 100 Baby, Not So Berry, Asylum, Decades) and **original concepts** designed to fill out each category. Roughly 13 per category.

---

## 1. Wealth & Progression

1. **Rags to Riches** — Start on an empty lot with §0 and no career; build a fortune from skills alone. The purest "from nothing" climb in the game.
2. **From Tenant to Tycoon** — Begin as a broke renter and end up owning and managing the whole block (For Rent). Every profit goes back into the next property.
3. **The One-Crop Empire** — Every Simoleon you ever earn must trace back to a single starting plant. Sell, reinvest, and grow an empire from one tomato.
4. **Self-Made Mogul** — Open a retail shop or restaurant while dead broke and grow it to a five-star business. No outside income allowed.
5. **Penny Pincher** — Live an entire lifetime never spending more than §50 on any single purchase. Wealth is built one careful coin at a time.
6. **The Yard-Sale Hustle** — Fund your whole life by collecting, flipping, and reselling found items. Nothing is bought new.
7. **Debt to Diamonds** — Start in deep simulated debt and claw your way up to a paid-off mansion. Track every repayment honestly.
8. **Off-the-Grid Tycoon** — Get genuinely rich while never once connecting to power or water. Comfort is earned the hard way.
9. **Garden of Gold** — Build your entire fortune through gardening, flower arranging, and the nectar trade. Green thumbs only.
10. **The Side-Hustle Stack** — Run five different income streams at once with no main career. Juggling them all is the real challenge.
11. **Homeless to Homeowner** — Start on a park bench with §0 and no shelter, and work up to your own deed. Survive first, thrive later.
12. **Single Simoleon Start** — Begin with exactly §1 and never touch a money cheat again. Every decision counts from rock bottom.
13. **Freelance Famine** — Survive only on freelance and gig work, with no stable salary ever. Feast-or-famine income is the whole point.

## 2. Family & Generational

1. **The Legacy Challenge** — Carry one bloodline through ten unbroken generations on a single founding lot. The genre-defining family marathon.
2. **100 Baby Challenge** — One matriarch line must raise 100 children to young adulthood, using as few matriarchs as possible. Endurance at its most chaotic.
3. **Not So Berry** — Each generation is themed around a colour, with set aspirations, careers, and traits to match. A creative spin on the classic legacy.
4. **Random Legacy** — A generator rolls each generation's career, traits, number of children, and special rules. You never know what's coming next.
5. **The Crowned Dynasty** — Rise from minor noble to the highest royal rank and hold the crown for three generations (Royalty & Legacy). Prestige is everything.
6. **Single Parent Scramble** — Raise a large family entirely solo, with no partner ever moving in to help. One Sim, many mouths.
7. **The Orphanage** — A single guardian raises a constantly rotating house full of adopted children. Kids age up and move on as new ones arrive.
8. **Black Sheep Legacy** — Each heir must be the deliberate opposite of their parent in career, traits, and lifestyle. The family never repeats itself.
9. **Generational Curse** — A defined misfortune passes down the bloodline; every heir must either break it or survive it. Drama is built in.
10. **The Wonder Child** — Raise one perfect child who maxes every childhood skill and completes their aspiration before aging up. Total focus on a single kid.
11. **Townie Takeover** — Over generations, marry into and inherit every major pre-made family in the world. End the game owning the whole town's bloodlines.
12. **Alphabet Legacy** — Name each generation's children in order down the alphabet, A to Z. A long, light-hearted naming constraint that shapes the whole save.
13. **The Founder's Vow** — Choose one strict family law (succession or marriage) and never break it across ten generations, even when it costs you. Your own rule is the antagonist.

## 3. Occult & Supernatural

1. **Vampire Black Widow** — An immortal vampire marries and outlives ten wealthy spouses while keeping their secret. Inheritance funds an eternal lifestyle.
2. **Full-Moon Pack Leader** — Become an Apex werewolf and grow a loyal pack while mastering your fury (Werewolves). Lose control in public and the run is marked.
3. **The Coven Rises** — Build a full household of spellcasters and take them all from apprentice to Virtuoso. Magic is the family business.
4. **Mermaid's Reef** — Live entirely by the ocean as a mermaid, never holding a land-based job. The sea provides everything.
5. **Alien Infiltration** — An alien must blend into town, abduct quietly, and secretly repopulate the neighbourhood with hybrids. Stay undetected at all costs.
6. **Monster Mash** — Raise one child of every occult type under a single roof. Balancing their needs and conflicts is the challenge.
7. **The Immortal** — Keep one Sim alive forever by any supernatural means available. Death is the only fail state.
8. **Cursed Bloodline** — An occult gene resurfaces every generation and can never be fully tamed. Each heir wrestles with their inherited nature.
9. **Hunter vs Hunted** — A Sim hunts occults across town while slowly, unavoidably becoming one themselves. The line between hunter and prey blurs.
10. **Spellcaster Duel Club** — Master magic and win a long streak of spellcaster duels without a single loss. Pure competitive sorcery.
11. **The Reluctant Vampire** — Survive as a vampire who refuses to ever drink directly from a Sim. Manage thirst through every other means possible.
12. **PlantSim Garden** — Live as a PlantSim sustained only by sunlight and water. A gentle, peaceful survival run.
13. **Ghost Manor** — Fill a haunted house, then play only the ghosts who remain after the living are gone. The afterlife becomes the main game.

---

## 4. Survival & Hardship

1. **Off-Grid Homestead** — Survive two full in-game years off the grid, growing or foraging every calorie (Seasons + Cottage Living). No shops, no deliveries.
2. **The Apocalypse Challenge** — Live under a long list of restrictions, each lifted only when you hit a specific milestone. Slowly rebuild civilisation rule by rule.
3. **Wasteland Wanderer** — Survive a ruined world on scavenged food, water, and shelter. Comfort is a luxury you have to earn.
4. **Stranded** — Dropped into the wilderness with nothing but your wits (Outdoor Retreat). Forage, fish, and build from scratch.
5. **The Last Winter** — Stockpile relentlessly through autumn to survive one long, brutal winter. Poor planning means starvation.
6. **Plague Quarantine** — Keep a sealed household alive through a spreading illness. Nobody leaves until it passes.
7. **Blackout** — Live a complete lifetime with no electricity, ever. Candles, cold food, and early nights.
8. **The Drought** — Water is scarce and must be collected or earned drop by drop. Every wasted use sets you back.
9. **One-Room Bunker** — An entire family survives confined to a single sealed room. Space management becomes life-or-death.
10. **Feral to Functional** — Start as a "wild" Sim with zero skills and slowly rejoin society. Learn to be a person from nothing.
11. **Island Castaway** — Settle a remote island lot and become fully self-reliant. The mainland may as well not exist.
12. **Hand-to-Mouth** — Never hold more than one day's food or §100 at any time. You live permanently on the edge.
13. **The Long Walk** — A homeless Sim crosses every world and never sleeps on the same lot twice. Keep moving to survive.

## 5. Villain & Dark Roleplay

1. **Black Widow** — Marry, inherit, and move on, leaving a trail of wealthy exes behind. The base-game classic of profitable heartbreak.
2. **The Crime Boss** — Build a criminal empire and rule the town from the shadows. Power through the disreputable path.
3. **The Saboteur** — Systematically ruin the happiness of every other household in the neighbourhood. Misery is the objective.
4. **Gold Digger** — Climb from poverty to luxury purely by marrying ever-richer Sims. Love is strictly a business strategy.
5. **The Manipulator** — Reach the top of a career using only mean and mischief social interactions. Charm is banned; scheming is mandatory.
6. **Evil Mastermind** — Complete the villain aspiration path while building a secret lair beneath your home. Full supervillain fantasy.
7. **The Heartbreaker** — Romance the entire town with no Sim ever discovering the others. Get caught and the streak resets.
8. **Town Tyrant** — Seize influence and use eco or community policies to make everyone's life worse. Bureaucratic villainy.
9. **The Grifter** — Fund a lavish lifestyle entirely through scams, theft, and dishonest gain. Never earn an honest Simoleon.
10. **Rival Houses** — Run two families sworn to destroy each other, switching between them. Every win for one is a loss for the other.
11. **The Cult Leader** — Recruit a devoted household that bends entirely to one charismatic founder. Loyalty above all.
12. **Revenge Arc** — A wronged Sim methodically dismantles the family that ruined them. A slow, satisfying downfall.
13. **Bad Influence** — Raise children specifically to become the town's worst citizens. Parenting, but evil.

## 6. Build & Create-a-Sim

1. **The §20k Challenge** — Build a complete, fully livable family home for under §20,000. Every Simoleon of the budget matters.
2. **One-Colour Build** — Design an entire house using a single colour palette throughout. Restraint becomes the creative driver.
3. **Room by Room** — Unlock and furnish one new room only when the household can actually afford it. The house grows with the family's fortunes.
4. **The Speed Build** — Fully build and furnish a home within 60 real-world minutes. Decisiveness over perfection.
5. **Tiny Living Test** — House a happy family of four in a Tiny or Micro home (Tiny Living). Every square is precious.
6. **The Fixer-Upper** — Buy the worst lot in town and renovate it to five-star condition. Transformation is the whole story.
7. **Genetics Roulette** — Fully randomise two Sims in CAS, marry them, and play their unpredictable children. You inherit whatever the dice give you.
8. **Trait Generator Run** — Let a random roll decide your Sim's traits, then play strictly to them. No swapping out the bad ones.
9. **The Glow-Up** — Take a deliberately rough starter Sim and transform them inside and out. Style, skills, and confidence all level up.
10. **Build From a Word** — Generate a random word and design a home themed entirely around it. Creativity under a strange constraint.
11. **Decorator for Hire** — Furnish ten different households to a given brief without altering their structure. Interior design as a job.
12. **The Family Tree CAS** — Build a believable three-generation family in CAS, with realistic genetics and resemblance. Storytelling before play.
13. **Architectural Era** — Build one home in each distinct historical architectural style. A portfolio across the ages.

## 7. Career & Skill

1. **Jack of All Trades** — Reach the top of every single career with one Sim across their lifetime. The ultimate completionist grind.
2. **Skill Completionist** — Max every skill in the game before your Sim dies. A race against aging.
3. **The Overachiever** — Complete ten full aspirations with a single Sim. Never stop chasing the next goal.
4. **Doctor's Orders** — Roleplay a complete medical career without cheating a single shift (Get to Work). Save lives the honest way.
5. **From Intern to Icon** — Climb the fame ladder starting unknown and broke (Get Famous). Build a reputation from zero.
6. **The Renaissance Sim** — Master one creative, one physical, and one mental skill to the very top. A well-rounded master of three worlds.
7. **Detective's Casebook** — Solve a long streak of cases without a single wrong arrest (Get to Work). Precision police work.
8. **Self-Employment Only** — Earn a living exclusively through freelance and self-employed skill paths. No boss, no salary.
9. **The Bestseller** — Write, publish, and top the in-game charts with a whole library of books. Build a literary legacy.
10. **Five-Star Chef** — Go from burnt eggs to a five-star restaurant empire (Dine Out). Master cooking, then management.
11. **The Athlete** — Take a Sim from out of shape to peak fitness and the top of the athletic career. Pure dedication arc.
12. **Master of One** — Pick a single skill and build an entire life and income around only it. Depth over breadth.
13. **Career Speedrun** — Reach the top of a chosen career in the fewest possible Sim-days. Efficiency is the game.

## 8. Themed & Narrative

1. **The Decades Challenge** — Live from the 1690s to today, following era-accurate rules for each decade. A sweeping march through history.
2. **Disney Princess Challenge** — Each generation recreates a different fairy-tale princess's story. Romance, hardship, and a happy ending per heir.
3. **The Asylum Challenge** — Eight wildly-trait'd Sims share a house, but you may only control one. Glorious, hands-off chaos.
4. **Runaway Teen** — A teen survives alone, dodging authorities until they legally come of age. Independence against the odds.
5. **History Reborn** — Play a strict historical era with no modern objects, careers, or technology. Immersion is the reward.
6. **Soap Opera** — Engineer maximum drama, affairs, and feuds across one tangled friend group. Pure melodrama by design.
7. **The Sitcom House** — Six unrelated roommates share one home under comedic house rules. Found-family hijinks.
8. **Desert Island Story** — A narrative castaway tale told across one Sim's stranded lifetime. Survival as storytelling.
9. **The Town That Time Forgot** — Restore an abandoned, run-down world back into a thriving community. Renewal lot by lot.
10. **Whodunit** — Stage a murder-mystery household where one secret "culprit" Sim must avoid suspicion. Role-play the intrigue.
11. **The Pioneer Trail** — A frontier family settles unclaimed land and survives the hardships of the journey. Grit and homesteading.
12. **Royal Court Drama** — Nobles scheme for favour and succession within a single palace (Royalty & Legacy). Intrigue over the throne.
13. **The Time Traveller** — One Sim "jumps" eras at every life stage, adapting completely to each. A life lived across centuries.

---

## 9. Bite-Sized & Competitive

1. **The 24-Hour Challenge** — Accomplish one clearly defined goal within a single in-game day. Quick, sharp, and replayable.
2. **Scenario Sprint** — Finish a built-in or custom scenario in one short sitting. Perfect for a lunch break.
3. **Speedrun to a Million** — Race to §1,000,000 as fast as possible by any legal means. The clock is the opponent.
4. **The Daily Dare** — A fresh single-task prompt each day, like "earn §500 before noon." Bite-sized and habit-forming.
5. **One-Week Wonder** — Take a Sim from nobody to a set milestone in just seven in-game days. Pressure-cooker progress.
6. **The Coin-Flip Life** — Every major decision is settled by a real coin or dice roll. Surrender control to chance.
7. **Last Sim Standing** — Start a full house and see who's still thriving after ten days. A low-effort survival contest.
8. **The Lottery Life** — Play only with whatever a random generator hands you each round. Adapt to your luck.
9. **No-Pause Challenge** — Complete a goal without ever pausing the game. Think and act in real time.
10. **Five-Minute Makeover** — Re-style a Sim and their room against a real-world timer. Fast creative reps.
11. **The Streak** — Complete a daily mini-goal every day for a fortnight without missing once. Consistency is the win.
12. **Bingo Board** — Fill a 5×5 board of random Sim tasks; first full line wins. A flexible, repeatable framework.
13. **The Gauntlet** — A fixed sequence of escalating mini-challenges played back to back. Endurance in a single sitting.

---

## 10. Pack-Driven

1. **Pack Legacy** — Build one generation entirely around each pack you own, in release order. Finally play everything you've bought.
2. **The Single-Pack Challenge** — A self-contained run using the features of exactly one pack. Perfect for newcomers and DLC deep-dives.
3. **Use It or Lose It** — Every session must use a mechanic from a pack you've never actually touched. Rediscover your own library.
4. **The Completionist's Tour** — Fully experience every world you own, one move at a time. A grand tour of the whole game.
5. **New Pack, New Life** — Start a fresh Sim built around each pack the moment it releases. Your save evolves with the game.
6. **Kit Collector** — Design rooms or looks using only one Build or CAS kit at a time. A constraint-driven style exercise.
7. **Expansion Marathon** — Complete the headline feature of each expansion you own, back to back. A victory lap across the big packs.
8. **The Forgotten DLC** — Pick the pack you've used least and build an entire challenge from it. Give the underdog its day.
9. **World Hopper** — Live one full life stage in every world before finally settling down. See the whole map through one family.
10. **Game Pack Gauntlet** — Clear the signature challenge of each game pack in sequence. A themed tour of the mid-size packs.
11. **Seasons of Change** — A year-long run that leans hard into every season's mechanics (Seasons). Weather drives the whole story.
12. **The Grand Tour** — Vacation in every getaway destination you own with one family. A lifetime of holidays.
13. **Everything Everywhere** — A mega-legacy that must meaningfully use all of your packs over its run. The ultimate test of a full library.
